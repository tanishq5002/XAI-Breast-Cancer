Interpretation of Grad-CAM

Grad-CAM (Gradient-weighted Class Activation Mapping) highlights regions of the histopathology image that contributed most to the model's prediction. These visualizations indicate where the convolutional neural network focused while making classification decisions.

Warmer colors such as red and yellow represent regions of higher importance, whereas cooler colors such as blue indicate lower influence. The overlay combines the heatmap with the original image to provide a clearer understanding of the model’s reasoning.

Key Insight: The highlighted regions represent tissue patterns that the model considers significant for distinguishing between benign and malignant samples.
